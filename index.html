A Journey By Train

Introduction:
A journey by train is very pleasunt and enjoyable. It welcomes people of all classes.A journey by train is very much joyful to me. It is a common journey in Bangladesh. A few days ago, I made a journey by train from Dhaka to Chittagong.

Occasion:
During the last summer vacation one of my friends invited us to his village house in Chittagong. So, I along with my parents went there by train.




Journey By Train Enjoyable Moment

Description:
On the fixed day, We reached the Kamlapur Railway station at 7:30 am. My father brought tickets. I took my seat by the window. The guard blew the whistle and the train begun to move on. It started with a ratting sound. The train was moving very fast. A gentle breeze entered into the compartment. I felt fresh and cold. Gradually the train speed incresed. I peeped outside . The train was passing through the green fields. I fed my eyes seemed to run behind. We saw the farmer working in the fields. Cattle were grazing here and there. The train stopped at a station. I brought boiled eggs, bananas, magazines etc from the hawkers. When the train started from the station,I saw the hills of Chittagong. On the right, I saw the vast sheet of water of the Bay of Bengal. Gradually the sun was setting in the western sky. The golden rays of the declining sun fell on the water. The whole water of Bay of Bengal turned raddish. The scenery was too much charming to give my mind into another fields. All those pleased me a lot. In the midst of my reveie, The train reached Chittagong at 5 pm. I got down from the train. I saw hills on both the side of the railway line. I found my friend who came to receive me. Then,We safely reached my friend's house by a taxi . Our returned journey was equally Pleasant one.

Conclusion:
The pleasure and enjoyable journey lasted for a few hours. The journey gave us much pleasure. The memory of the journey will remain ever fresh in my heart. I never forgot this experience.
